FAQ

can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Go to one of the printers that are located in different locations in the building. Choose what you want to print and make sure that there is enough balance on your HZ student card because you need that to be able to print. When you are done printing, log out again.



can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Place your document on the glass plate of the scanner. Use your HZ pass again to scan. When your document is in the right place you can start scanning and press the start button. When you are done scanning you log out again.



How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

You can buy something through the HZ shop by going to the HZ shop and picking out something you want. When you have found everything you can order it.



How can you book a project space?

go to HZ.Portal. Go to the home page of the Self Service Portal. Click on Reserve Choose the correct category and search for the "resource" you want to reserve.If necessary, use the Type filter to turn certain items on/off in the overview. Select the day when the resource is borrowed Indicate, by dragging with the left mouse button, the time period when the resource is borrowed Click on Next Read the loan conditions, check the reservation and click on Next Fill in the necessary information and click on Confirm reservation



What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

When you arrive at the parking lot you can use your HZ pass to enter. If you do not have a HZ pass with you, you cannot enter.
