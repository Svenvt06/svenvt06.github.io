How can you book a project space?

go to HZ.Portal. Go to the home page of the Self Service Portal. Click on Reserve Choose the correct category and search for the "resource" you want to reserve.If necessary, use the Type filter to turn certain items on/off in the overview. Select the day when the resource is borrowed Indicate, by dragging with the left mouse button, the time period when the resource is borrowed Click on Next Read the loan conditions, check the reservation and click on Next Fill in the necessary information and click on Confirm reservation
