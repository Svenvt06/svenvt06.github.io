Personal SWOT analysis

Strength

helpful

I am very helpful and am always willing to help people if I can. This can always be useful in group assignments.

weakness

distracting

I am always quite easily distracted by things happening around me or things I could be doing other than what I am doing. The best thing for me is to be in a space where I can't do much else.

oppertunities

energy

I think it is a fun study to do and therefore I have enough willpower to complete it and that can be a nice help when I have to do something for school.

threats

procrastinating

Whenever there is a deadline for something I always do something that is fun to do but that should not be done. This can be quite dangerous if I can't keep up with it all later so it's up to me to prevent that.
